CONCERNtrated

Video & Photography
2018
CONCERNtrated is an experimental art work I made as a fine art student.
This short film with 9 photographs (documents my feelings and interpretations regarding social phobias.) is an interpretation of my feelings on suffering social phobia. It reveals the fear, the anxious emotions and the negative assumptions of being focused on by other people.
