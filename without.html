Without sense of?

Digital Painting
2017
This is a graduate assistant task of creating works to decorate the drawing & painting classroom. The inspiration for this set of works comes from my teaching observation of the students' learning process of painting during a descriptive painting course. Any painting can be summarized into three aspects:  color, shape, and light value. When one of the above aspects is in an extreme state, what obvious characteristics will be produced in the painting？I used the control variable method to make these digital paintings in order to create a reference.
